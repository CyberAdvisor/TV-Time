July 17, 2026Public launch

My Shows is now hosted at myshows.fyi

  • Public launch of the hosted version — free to use, no account required, nothing to install or host yourself.
  • Track shows by searching TVmaze's free database and marking the last episode you watched.
  • Shows are grouped into Available now, Upcoming, Pending return, and Completed, with countdowns for upcoming episodes.
  • Specials (Christmas specials, documentary episodes, etc.) are placed correctly in the timeline by air date.
  • Episode detail screen shows air date, runtime, episodes left in the current season, and seasons remaining.
  • Suspend a series without deleting it — it moves to Completed as Archived, with your last watched episode, remaining count, and the date recorded, and can be reactivated at any time.
  • Manual backup and restore, since this page doesn't use accounts or a server.
